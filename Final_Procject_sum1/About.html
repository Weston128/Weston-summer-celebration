Everything About Me

My name is Weston Haveman and I'm 14 years old going into my first year of highschool. My journey so far has been marked by a relentless pursuit of growth, particularly in academics where I've already skipped a math grade and am now heading into Honors Algebra 2. This dedication to learning also secured my acceptance into The Hidden Genius Project, a summer program that promises exciting new challenges. Beyond the classroom, I'm a passionate soccer player with a lifelong love for the game, having celebrated a significant victory in a challenging tournament with my team. My quick thinking extends to Rubik's Cubes, where my unofficial personal best is a lightning-fast 12 seconds, with an official competition time of 22 seconds. When I'm not on the field or solving puzzles, you can often find me expanding my collection of sneakers; I currently own five pairs of Jordans.
